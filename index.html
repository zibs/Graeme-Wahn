GRAEME WAHN

Working the reproductions of images, Graeme Wahn exploits and expands the photographic medium, creating circumstances in which the photograph interacts with itself amid surrounding structures. Consequently, common tropes from the history of photography evert - turn inside out on themselves – becoming uncanny, or something.

Graeme Wahn received a BFA in Photography from Emily Carr University of Art & Design in the spring of 2015. Although invested in the medium of photography, his work has recently begun to consider the image’s capacity to move through both sculptural and installation based practices. He continues to live and work in Vancouver where he maintains an active studio practice.
